--- layout: page title: Terms date: 2009-09-04 18:26:30 --- - Bend – fastening two ropes together - Bight, Loop – a loop in a rope - Bitter End – the inside end of a rope in use - Cleat, Fid – an object to affix a rope to - Eye – a loop - Grommet – a ring - Hitch – attaching a rope to an object - Knot – the forming of a knob in a rope - Lash – binding poles together - Splice – interweaving strands of rope rather than whole lines - Standing Part – the inactive part of the rope
